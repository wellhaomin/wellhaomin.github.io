__1.JIDU__  
<img src="/images/ROBO-01.jpg" alt="ROBO-01" title="ROBO-01" width="800" >  

JiDu's autonomous driving system is equipped with NVIDIA's dual Orin X chips and 31 external vehicle sensors, including 2 lidar, 5 millimeter-wave radar, 12 ultrasonic radar, and 12 cameras. Jidu also premiered the Qualcomm Snapdragon Automotive Digital Cockpit Platform - 8295 chip, which supports seamless integration with ultra-clear large screens and 3D visual effects for various scenarios such as driving navigation, gaming, entertainment, and online office needs.
